Mechanics

Mechanics studies motion, forces, energy, and momentum. It is based on Newton’s laws of motion.

  • Kinematics (motion)
  • Dynamics (forces)
  • Work and energy
  • Momentum and collisions

Electricity and Magnetism

This topic studies electric charge, current, voltage, and magnetic fields.

  • Ohm’s Law
  • Electric circuits
  • Capacitors
  • Magnetic fields

Optics

Optics studies light and its behavior: reflection, refraction, lenses, mirrors, and waves.

  • Reflection
  • Refraction
  • Lenses and mirrors
  • Wave optics

Thermodynamics

Thermodynamics deals with heat, temperature, energy transfer, and the laws of thermodynamics.

  • Temperature
  • Heat transfer
  • First law of thermodynamics
  • Heat engines

Modern Physics

Modern physics includes quantum mechanics, relativity, atomic and nuclear physics.

  • Quantum mechanics
  • Atoms and nuclei
  • Relativity
  • Particles
